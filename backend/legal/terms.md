# Aangan Terms of Use

**Version 2026-07-23 · Draft for pilot use — review by counsel before public launch.**

## 1. The service
Aangan provides a private family voice journal, an AI Companion that answers
questions **only from content each member chose to share**, care nudges, and
human-approved action preparation. AI outputs can be imperfect; they are
suggestions, not professional advice.

## 2. Not medical, legal, or emergency advice
Aangan's nudges and answers help family members connect. They are never
medical advice, diagnosis, or an emergency service. If someone may be in
danger, contact local emergency services.

## 3. Your account and responsibilities
You are responsible for your account and for what you record. You must be 18
or older during the pilot. Do not use Aangan to harass, monitor, or harm
others; do not attempt to access another member's private content; do not
misuse the action-preparation feature.

## 4. Sharing and consent
Sharing is always the author's choice. Standing rules you create are your
choice and can be turned off at any time. You may leave a circle at any time.

## 5. Actions
Prepared actions (for example a shopping cart) always stop before payment or
sending. You review, approve, and complete them yourself. Aangan never enters
payment or login details on your behalf.

## 6. Content and ownership
Your journal content is yours. You grant Aangan only the processing needed to
provide the service (transcription, summarisation, answering, indexing) as
described in the Privacy Policy. AI-generated summaries are provided as-is.

## 7. Cancellation and deletion
You can stop using Aangan at any time, export your data, delete entries, or
delete your account entirely.

## 8. Service quality
The pilot is provided as-is, without uptime guarantees. We may change features
with notice. Liability is limited to the maximum extent permitted by law.
